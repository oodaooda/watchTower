# M2 - 10Y Real Yield

Last validated: 2026-05-17

## What It Measures

10-Year Treasury Inflation-Indexed Security real yield from FRED.

## Source URL + Auth

- Source: FRED series `DFII10`
- URL: `https://api.stlouisfed.org/fred/series/observations`
- Auth: `FRED_API_KEY`

## Refresh Cadence

Daily after FRED updates. Scheduler target is weekday evening.

## Interpretation

Higher real yields can pressure long-duration technology valuations, including VGT-heavy exposure.

## Threshold Rules

- Green: `< 2.0%`
- Amber: `2.0%-2.5%`
- Red: `>= 2.5%`

## Known Failure Modes

- FRED returns `"."` for missing observation values.
- FRED latest date may lag market close.
- Directional interpretation can be context-dependent; use with VIX and credit spreads.
