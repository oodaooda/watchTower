# M1 - HY OAS

Last validated: 2026-05-17

## What It Measures

ICE BofA US High Yield Option-Adjusted Spread from FRED, converted from percent to basis points.

## Source URL + Auth

- Source: FRED series `BAMLH0A0HYM2`
- URL: `https://api.stlouisfed.org/fred/series/observations`
- Auth: `FRED_API_KEY`

## Refresh Cadence

Daily after FRED updates. Scheduler target is weekday evening.

## Interpretation

Higher spreads indicate tighter credit conditions and more market stress. Lower spreads indicate calmer credit conditions.

## Threshold Rules

- Green: `< 350` bps
- Amber: `350-450` bps
- Red: `> 450` bps

## Known Failure Modes

- FRED returns `"."` for missing observation values.
- FRED latest date may lag market close.
- API key missing or invalid.
