# E1 - News Sentiment Top 5

Last validated: 2026-05-17

## What It Measures

Average sentiment score of the five highest absolute sentiment Alpha Vantage news items for the VGT/top-tech watch basket.

## Source URL + Auth

- Source: Alpha Vantage `NEWS_SENTIMENT`
- URL: `https://www.alphavantage.co/query?function=NEWS_SENTIMENT`
- Auth: `ALPHA_VANTAGE_API_KEY`

## Refresh Cadence

Every 15 minutes during regular market hours in the target design.

## Interpretation

Positive values indicate more positive recent news sentiment. Negative values indicate more negative recent news sentiment. This is a monitoring signal, not a trade recommendation.

## Threshold Rules

- Green: absolute score `< 0.15`
- Amber: absolute score `>= 0.15`
- Red: score `<= -0.25`

## Known Failure Modes

- Alpha Vantage rate limits.
- News feed may contain duplicate or low-relevance articles.
- Sentiment scores can be noisy or stale.
